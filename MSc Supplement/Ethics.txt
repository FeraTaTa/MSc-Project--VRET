Participants must not suffer from any diagnosed phobias in relation to the material experienced (i.e. height phobias etc.). 

It must be disclosed to participants - before the experience starts - what potential experiences/effects they may be encounter (i.e. experiences in relation to heights etc.) so they know what to expect in terms of the user experience. 

The study must be cancelled (for the participant) if a participant feels undue discomfort/fear etc. 

The supervisor must approve all material before the study can commence.
